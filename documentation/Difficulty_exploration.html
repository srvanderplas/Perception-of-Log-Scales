Exponential: Multiplicative

For \[y = \alpha(\beta x + \epsilon) + \theta\] with

\[\begin{align*} xRange &= (0, 30)\\ N &= 20\\ \alpha &= 1\\ \theta &= 0\\ \epsilon &\sim N(0, \text{sdErr}) \end{align*}\]

where \(\text{sdErr} = 1\%\) of \(y\) range.

beta sdErr Difficulty
